<details>
  <summary>To remove the light assemblies:</summary>
  1.  Turn the robot's circuit breaker off.
  2.  Remove the _Front Panel_.
  3.  Remove the _Rear Panel_.
  4.  Disconnect the light's cable.
  5.  Remove the 2 nuts that fasten the metal brackets to the chassis.
  6.  Remove the light subassemblies from the robot.

  :::note

  When reinstalling the light assembly:

  * Torque each light assembly's 2 nuts to 2 N·m.

  :::

</details>

<details>
  <summary>To remove the PCBA from the assembly:</summary>
  1.  Remove the 2 flat head screws from each of the plastic diffusers.
  2.  Remove the diffusers and cowls.
  3.  Remove the PCBA.

  :::note

  When reinstalling the PCBA:

  * Torque each of the diffusers's 2 screws to 0.3 N·m.

  :::

</details>
