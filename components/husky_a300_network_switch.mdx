### Network Switch

[//]: <> (TODO, add image of the Network Swith inside the robot)

The network switch connects the primary computer, the rear debigging port, and any additional Ethernet devices.

<details>
  <summary>To remove the switch:</summary>
  
  1.  Shut off the robot's circuit breaker.
  2.  Remove Husky's _Top Plate_.
  3.  Remove any network cables from the switch.
  4.  Remove Husky's _Front Panel_.
  5.  Remove the 4 screws and waterproofing washers, while holding onto the switch.
      
      [//]: <> (TODO, add image of the 4 screws and tool)

  6.  Disconnect the switch's power cable, and remove it from the robot.

      [//]: <> (TODO, add switch's power cable at the back of the switch)

  :::note

    When reinstalling the switch:

    * Make sure the power cable is connected.
    * Make sure the waterfroofing washers are included.
    * Torque the 4 screws to 1.2 N·m.

  :::

</details>
