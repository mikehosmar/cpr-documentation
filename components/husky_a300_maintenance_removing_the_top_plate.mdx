### Removing The Top Plate

[//]: <> (TODO, add an image of the top plate being removed.)

To remove Husky's _Top Plate_:

1.  Turn off the Husky.
2.  Open the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
3.  Flip the _Circuit Breaker_ to the Off position.
4.  Close the _Rear Charge Port Door.
6.  Remove the 7 screws from the _Top Plate_.
    Note that one of the screws also has a sealing washer attached to it.
7.  If applicable, remove any of your custom cables from the Top Plate's connector openings. 
8.  Lift the _Top Plate_ off of the robot.

:::danger

Do not drag the _Top Plate_ on the robot, because it may damage the bulb seal around the Husky's chassis.

:::
