### Computer

There are 2 computer mounting locations on Husky's _Electronics Tray_.
Both mounting locations are sized for Mini-ITX computers.

:::tip

Clearpath offers a standard computer with an Intel i3 processor, or a performance option with a i7 processor—though custom builds are also an option.

:::

<details>
  <summary>To remove a computer:</summary>
  1.  Turn the robot's circuit breaker off.
  2.  Remove Husky's _Top Plate_.
  3.  Disconnect any cables from the computer:
      1.  USB.
      2.  Ethernet RJ45.
      3.  HDMI.
      4.  Wi-Fi antennas.
      5.  Power cable.
      6.  Optional graphics card's PCIe riser cable.
  4.  Remove the 4 screws fastning the computer to Husky's _Electronics Tray_.
  5.  Remove the computer from the robot.

  :::note

  When reinstalling the computer:

  * Torque the 4 screws to 1 N·m.

  :::

  :::note

  If you are changing the Wi-Fi or Storage M.2 cards:

  * Torque these screws to 0.3 N·m.
