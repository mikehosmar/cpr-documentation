### Nameplate And Regulatory

Husky's _Nameplate_ is located under the _Front Cover_.
The _Nameplate_ includes regulatory marks, the product's name, serial number, and date of manufacture.

There will be other labels on the chassis with FCC information, such as the Intel Wi-Fi card shown in the attached image.
