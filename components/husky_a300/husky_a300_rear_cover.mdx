<details>
  <summary>To remove the _Rear Cover_</summary>
  
  1.  Remove the 2 screws and washers.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_rear_cover_2.png"
            width="600"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>

  2.  Lift the cover's pins from the chassis's 2 rubber grommets.
      Keep the _Rear Charge Port Door_ closed while removing the _Rear Cover_.

  :::note

  When reinstalling the cover:

  * Make sure the washers are included with the screws.
  * Torque the tray's 2 screws to 2 N·m.

  :::

</details>
