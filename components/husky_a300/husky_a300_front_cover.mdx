### Front Cover {#removing-the-front-cover}

<center>
  <figure>
    <img

The Husky's identification labels, status lights, air filter, and some fasteners are located under the _Front Cover_.

<details>
  <summary>To remove this cover:</summary>

  <ol>
    <li>
      Twist the 2 thumb-latches 90°, so the arrows are horizontal.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_front_cover_2.jpg"
            width="700"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>
    </li>
    <li>
      Tilt the cover.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_front_cover_3.png"
            width="700"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>
    </li>
    <li>
      Lift the cover's pins from the chassis's 2 rubber grommets.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_front_cover_4.png"
            width="700"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>
    </li>
  </ol>

  :::danger Reinstall Before Use

  The cover must be installed during operations to maintain Husky's IP54 ingress protection rating, and reduce the risk of entrapment.

  :::

</details>
