### Lifting, By Hand

[//]: <> (TODO, add image of the robot being lifted by 2 people)

:::safety-warning

Never attempt a 1-person lift of Husky A300.

:::

<br/>

:::safety-caution

Follow your local workplace regulations for maximum allowable weight for 2 person lifts.
Your Husky configuration's mass is listed in the [Technical Specifications](#technical-specifications)].

:::

<br/>

:::safety-warning

Never use the bumper bars to lift Husky A300 with mounted payloads.

This may exceed the lift rating for the bumper bars. It may also exceed the safe limit for a 2-person lift. Use lifting straps instead.

:::
