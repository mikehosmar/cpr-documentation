The Husky configurations with 40 Ah and 80 Ah batteries include a metal tray inside the chassis.
You can mount your custom integration components on this tray.

:::note

The 120 Ah Husky fills the entire chassis volume with batteries.

:::

<details>
  <summary>To remove the tray:</summary>

  1.  Remove the _Electronics Tray_, as it is blocking access to the battery volume.
  2.  Remove the _Battery Integration Tray's_ screws
      * 3X screws for the 40 Ah Husky
      * 2X screws for the 80 Ah Husky
  3.  Remove the tray
