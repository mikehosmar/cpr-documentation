### Unboxing the Husky A300  {#unboxing-a300}

To remove the Husky A300 from its crate:

1.  Remove the bolts securing the front panel of the crate.
    You will need a 14 mm hex socket for this. 
    <center>
      <img
        src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_crate_closed.png"
        width="400"
      />
    </center>
1. Position the front panel as a ramp at the bottom of the crate.
    <center>
      <img
        src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_crate_open.png"
        width="400"
      />
    </center>
1. Loosen and remove the retaining straps.
1. Roll the Husky A300 out of the crate.
