### Turning The Robot On  {#turning-the-robot-on}

To turn the Husky on:

1.  Check that the _Circuit Breaker_ is in the ON position.
2.  Press and hold the _Power Button_ for 0.5 seconds.

:::info

Press and hold the _Power Button_ for 0.5 seconds to turn the Husky off.
Holding the button for longer will not force the robot to shut down faster.

:::
