### AMP's Cable Routings {#cable-routings}

The AMP's enclosure has brushes on both ends, allowing you to route cables outside the enclosure.
There are 3 layers of brushes per side, to limit water ingress.
We weave cables through the brushes sideways, to prevent a straight water ingress point.

:::tip

Test your integration for ingress points by placing a light inside the enclosure, and then looking for light bleed near cables.
Adjust your routings till you are confident the integration will prevent water splash ingress in accordance with IPx4.

:::

:::info

The enclosure has threaded holes to mount an [Icotek KEL-U 24&#124;10 V2A _(54241.200)_](https://ca.rs-online.com/product/icotek/54241-200/72429591/).
This Icotek housing replaces the AMP enclosure's 3 layers of brushes.
You can then use Icotek split grommets to retain cables.

This is not required for most IP54 applications.
Brushes are the default configuration for Husky AMP as they simplify integrations.

:::
