### Escalator brushes

Both ends of the Husky AMP's enclosure include 3 rows of escalator brushes.
We weave cables through these brushes on an angle, to keep water and dust out of the enclosure.
To add cables through these brushes:

1.  Get a plastic ruler or other flat non-conductive tool.
2.  Slide the plastic ruler through the brushes sideways.
3.  Twist the ruler 90°.
4.  Pass the cable through the opening in the brushes.
5.  Remove the plastic ruler.
6.  Insect the cable pass through, ensuring there are not large openings for water splashes to enter through.
    Adjust the brushes's bristles if nescessary.
