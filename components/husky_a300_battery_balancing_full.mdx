### Battery Cell Balancing _(Full)_

Refer to the notes above regarding the [weekly "quick" cell balancing](#quick-cell-balance).

A full balance of 24+ hours is recommended at least once per month.

Quick Battery Cell Balance Procedure:

1.  Place the robot on a level surface.
2.  Power off the robot.
3.  Confirm the robot's circuit breaker is in the On position.
4.  Connect the manual charger.
5.  Let the batteries charge for at least 24 hours.
