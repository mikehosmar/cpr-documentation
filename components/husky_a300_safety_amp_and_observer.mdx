:::note

All the risks noted in the [Husky A300's user manual](/docs_robots/outdoor_robots/husky/a300/user_manual_husky/#important-safety-information) also apply to Husky AMP and Husky Observer.

:::

:::safety-danger[Crushing / Impact]

**Never rely on the Husky AMP or Husky Observer as the only method to avoid collisions.**

While extensive testing has been performed to validate system robustness, autonomous operation of Husky AMP and Husky Observer, they are not safety rated in accordance with ISO 13849 or similar standards.
The Husky AMP and Husky Observer are intended for research and development purposes.
The Husky AMP and Husky Observer are not intended for use in deployments where risks are mitigated exclusively through the use of the robot.
The user is responsible for performing their own Risk Assessment, to ensure the Husky AMP and Husky Observer can be operated safely in the user's operating environment.

Contact [Support](#support) for details on how to upgrade to a safety rated autonomy system for Husky A300, Husky AMP, and Husky Observer.

:::
