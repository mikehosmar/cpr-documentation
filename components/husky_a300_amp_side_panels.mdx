### Side Panels

[//]: <> (TODO, add an image of the side panels being removed.)

The Husky AMP's enclosure has two removable panels.
To remove these panels:

1.  Turn off the Husky.
2.  Open the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
3.  Flip the _Circuit Breaker_ to the Off position.
4.  Close the _Rear Charge Port Door_.
5.  Remove the 4 fastener covers from the enclosure.
    These covers are held on with friction, so just pull them by hand.
6.  Remove the 4 nuts from the cover.
7.  Lift the _Side Panel_ from the Husky's enclosure.

:::info

When reinstalling the _Side Panels_:

* Make sure the 4 spaces are still touching the enclosure.
  These spacers set the weather seal's compression height.
* Torque the 4 nuts to 3 N·m.

:::
