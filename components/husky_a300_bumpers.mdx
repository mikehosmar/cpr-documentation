<details>
  <summary>To remove the bumpers:</summary>

  1.  Remove the 2 screws that hold the bumper to the Husky.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_bumpers_2.png"
            width="700"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>

  2.  Gather the 2 screws, and 4 coped spacers per bumper.
      <center>
        <figure>
          <img
            src="/img/robot_images/husky_a300_images/husky_a300_bumpers_3.png"
            width="700"
          />
        </figure>
      </center>

  :::info

  When reinstalling the bumpers:

  * Make sure 2 coped spacers are included per screw.
  * Torque the screw to 22 N·m.

  :::

</details>
